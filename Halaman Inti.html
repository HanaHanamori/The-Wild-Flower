About Pramudita

Hello, my name is Pramudita, but people often called me Hanamori or Hana. I am an illustrator, comic artist, and graphic designer. My love for illustration, comic, and design has been always keeping me up since i child. I love exploring and surfing through unique ideas. With colorful and detailed style, I always try to show people my style inside of my artwork. You can see a lot of my artwork here and feel free to contact me if you are interested in my art.
